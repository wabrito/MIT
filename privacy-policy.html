Privacy Policy

🔒No Personal Data Collected

TRM Colombia does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. No account, login, or registration is required to use the app.

🌐Network Requests

The app fetches publicly available exchange rate data from the following sources. No user data is included in these requests.

  • datos.gov.co — Official Colombian government open data (TRM)
  • cdn.jsdelivr.net — Public currency API (EUR/USD rate)

💾Local Storage

Exchange rate data is cached locally on your device to reduce network usage. This cached data contains only exchange rates and dates — no personal information of any kind.

🚫No Third-Party Tracking

TRM Colombia does not use any of the following:

Analytics SDKs Advertising Crash reporting User tracking Social logins
