Privacy Policy — TRM Colombia Last updated: April 13, 2026 TRM Colombia ("the App") is a personal utility app that displays the official Colombian peso exchange rate (TRM) against USD and EUR. Data Collection The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No account registration is required. Network Requests The App fetches publicly available exchange rate data from: datos.gov.co (Colombian government open data) cdn.jsdelivr.net (public currency API) No user data is sent to these services beyond a standard HTTP request. Local Storage Exchange rate data is cached locally on your device to avoid redundant network requests. This data contains no personal information. Third Parties The App does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking. Contact wabrito@gmail.com
